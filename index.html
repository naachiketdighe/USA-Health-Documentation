Motivation

This motivation behind the USA Health data dashboard it to provide an interactive tool to help understand important relationships between various factors such as the percentage of people living in poverty that are also smokers or percentage of people that smoke and the air quality surrounding them.

Visualization components:

Attribute Select







Spatial visualization via Choropleth Map



Interesting Findings from the Application:





  • People that fall in the lower to the middle of the Median household income have the highest percentage of no health insaurance. This data represents that health insaurance has become so expensive that even the average earners are unable to afford it.




  • Most national parks are located in the Utah/Washington area, as is evident from this map.
